Tips for negotiating rent in Bangalore's current market

Solved 1.2k views 24 replies
RaviKumar
Original Poster
Feb 15, 2025, 10:30 AM

I've been looking at apartments in Bangalore and wanted to share some effective negotiation strategies that worked for me:

  1. Research average rents in the area using multiple property websites
  2. Visit the property during different times of the day
  3. Build rapport with the owner and highlight your credentials
  4. Be prepared to commit to a longer lease for better rates
  5. Ask about including maintenance and amenities in the rent

Has anyone else successfully negotiated their rent recently? What strategies worked for you?

Great tips, Ravi! I'd also add that timing is crucial. I found that looking for apartments during the off-season (October-November) helped me get better deals. The demand is lower during these months.

Another strategy that worked for me was offering to pay a few months' rent in advance. Many owners appreciate this and are willing to negotiate the monthly rate.
